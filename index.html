About

Why “Flawing Forward”?

It’s a twist on “failing forward.” Failing forward says mistakes are survivable. Flawing forward says the flaws are the point — that you move because of them, not in spite of them. Perfect was never the goal. Forward is.

For most of my life I was the one with a folder full of almost-finished things — work that was good enough to share, quietly dying on a hard drive. I called it high standards. It was fear. Flawing Forward is the promise I made to myself: make imperfect things, put them into the world anyway, and help other people do the same.

If you’ve been waiting until you’re ready — ready is a myth. Let’s flaw forward together.
